Welcome

Welcome to the Coldplay fan website, featuring information, tour dates, videos and everything you need to know.

Coldplay is an English pop rock band formed in London in 1996. The band's members are Chris Martin, Jonny Buckland, Guy Berryman and Will Champion.

MUSIC OF THE SPHERES

Music of the Spheres is the 9th studio album by Coldplay. It was released on October 15 in 2021. The new album was produced by Max Martin and it features guest appearances from Selena Gomez, We Are King, Jacob Collier and BTS.
